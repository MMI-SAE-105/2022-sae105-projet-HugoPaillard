About me

Hello, my name is Hugo Paillard, I am currently in the first year of BUT MMi (Multimedia and Internet professions) in Montbéliard (25). The site you are currently browsing is the project carried out during the first semester.

The purpose of this site is to speak to a "me" of the future, by introducing him to a film, a book, a music and a place that I love, as well as a regret and a hope of my generation.

I therefore wish you an excellent moment within my universe, and that you are as affected as my future "me" by reading these articles.
